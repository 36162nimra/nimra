IBM

IBM is a global technology company that provides hardware, software, cloud-based services, and cognitive computing. Founded in 1911, IBM is known for its mainframe computers, Watson AI platform, and consulting services.

IBM Watson

IBM Watson is an AI platform that uses natural language processing and machine learning to reveal insights from large amounts of unstructured data. It has applications in healthcare, finance, and other industries.

IBM Products

IBM offers a range of products, including servers, storage systems, software, and cloud services. It also provides consulting and IT services to help businesses with digital transformation.
